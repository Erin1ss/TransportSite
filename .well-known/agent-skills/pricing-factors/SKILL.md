# Pricing Factors

Use this skill when an agent needs to explain what affects the public route estimate for ЕдемЛежа.

## Factors

- Route: city trip, Tatarstan route, or intercity travel.
- Floor and elevator: elevator availability, stairs, narrow spans, and distance from door to vehicle.
- Carrying support: whether lifting or descent without elevator is needed.
- Waiting: clinic, CT, MRI, procedure, consultation, or return trip waiting time.
- Timing: planned route, urgent same-day request, night route, or fixed appointment time.
- Passenger specifics: wheelchair, postoperative condition, limited mobility, and baggage or documents.

The site does not publish universal fixed prices because routes and transfer conditions vary. Use the contact page or phone number for a route-specific estimate.
